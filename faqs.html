Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order?

Visit our online store, browse through our products, and add items to your cart. Follow the checkout process to place your order.

2. Can I modify or cancel my order?

Once an order is placed, modifications or cancellations may not be possible. Please contact our support team for assistance.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment options. Check our payment information during checkout for details.

4. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. Use this number to track your order on our website or the shipping carrier's site.

5. What is your return policy?

Refer to our Return Policy for detailed information on returns, exchanges, and refunds.

Contact us at info@rmskart.com for any questions or concerns.
